Why LiveramSolutions?

A word from the creator

After a corporate restructuring in 2023 led me to look for a change from my long time professional career in operations and logistics as well as being involved in various support rolls including IT and solution based problem solving, I found myself working in a new environment with different resources and challenges.

Driven by a simple frustration—repeatedly opening office programs each day to perform the same calculations. In a workplace where most tools are already browser-based, it made far more sense to bring those spreadsheet functions into a webpage that could stay open and easily accessible at all times, and in 2024 I conceived LiveramSolutions.

From there, the idea grew. After speaking with family and friends, a question emerged: what useful tools could be created to help others? More importantly, what services currently sit behind paywalls that could instead be offered freely?

That question set everything in motion.

It also sparked a broader reflection on how software used to be—simpler, more accessible, and more user-focused. Freeware was genuinely free, or you paid once and owned it for life. Programs were purpose-built, straightforward, and designed to do a specific job well. There were no subscriptions, no constant upgrade fees for features you may never need, and no requirement to sign up and hand over personal information just to get started. Most importantly, software ran on your device and respected your privacy.

LiveramSolutions aims to bring a bit of that philosophy back.

While I am not part of the IT or tech industry, and my skills are self-developed, the tools available today make it possible to turn practical ideas into real, usable applications. This site is a result of that—built with the goal of creating simple, effective tools that anyone can access and benefit from.

At its core, this project is about helping. If even one person finds a tool here that solves a problem or saves time—even just once—then it has achieved its purpose.

A sincere thank you to my wife and family for their encouragement and inspiration, and to you for taking the time to visit, read this, and hopefully make use of what’s been created here.

All the best, LiveramSolutions
