Oh Hell! Playing Guide

The Trump Card

Before each hand, a Trump Suit is selected. This suit is the most powerful for that round. "NT" stands for No Trump, meaning only the highest card of the suit led can win.

The Rules of Play

  • The Hook: The dealer (last to bid) cannot bid a number that makes the total bids equal the cards in hand.
  • Scoring: Exactly match your bid for a 10-point bonus + 1 per trick. Miss your bid, and you get 0 bonus points.
